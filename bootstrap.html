Exchange Training

The 40th edition of the "Joint Combined Exchange Training" between the Nepali Army and the United States Army is going to start from September 10. The training will be held in Kathmandu Valley until September 29, according to NA Directorate of Military Public Relations and Information

Mobilize Nepal Army

The government is going to forward a proposal for army mobilization before President Bidya Devi Bhandari as per the Cabinet decision to mobilize Nepal Army (NA) personnel in coronavirus contaminated zones
